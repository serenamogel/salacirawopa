What time is the daily reset in Genshin Impact?

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 27, 2024
Recommended Videos

Everything runs on a clock in Genshin Impact, especially the dungeons that rotate out loot every day. Not every item you need to Ascend weapons or abilities is available all the time, though there are alternatives available when you do need it. Regardless, when you need to wait a day for something to show up, it’s better to use Original Resin to dive into a dungeon. What time does the game reset all of the dungeons and various loot drops that you can grab all over again?

It varies on the time zone of the server you have your character. The Genshin team officially shared this information for players who wanted to make sure they could be on top of everything, especially those who played at certain times. The answer was shared on the Genshin Impact Reddit page. It breaks down to GMT-5 for North America servers, GMT+1 for the European Union servers, and GMT+8 for Asia servers.

So long as you keep these factors in mind, you should be able to jump on the right date or do a last-minute dungeon before the end of the day. For those in the United States, that’s not too bad, and it means that late-night sessions are certainly possible, with the potential for a new loot drop to happen if they stay up late enough. To make sure the transition happens in Genshin Impact, you probably have to restart when the servers need to make the changes.
