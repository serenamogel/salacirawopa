Actress Surekha Sikri Passed Away Who Are Her Husband And Son?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, May 3, 2024

Surekha Sikri is survived only by her son Rahul Sikri. Her husband passed away over a decade ago.

Popular actress and TV personality, Surekha Sikri, has passed away. She had been feeling ill for several past months and ultimately lost her life due to cardiac arrest.

The award-winning star was surrounded by her friends, family, and caregivers.

Explore Surekha Sikri on Wikipedia

Surekha Sikri also has a Wikipedia biography. 

A native of Uttar Pradesh, she spent her early days in Nanital and Almora. Her father was in the Indian airforce and her mother worked as a teacher. Likewise, Surekha Sikri was also a family member to Naseeruddin Shah as her brother-in-law.

She attended the National School of Drama and also worked for the Repertory Company. Eventually, she moved to Mumbai to pursue her film career.

Over the years, the actress starred in dozens of TV series and movies. As a fact, she won a total of three National Film Awards as Best Supporting Actress for films like Mammo (1995), Tamas (1988), and Badhaai Ho (2018).

Recently, she was seen in Sheer Qorma (2000) and Ghost Stories (2020).

Who is Surekha Sikri Son? Meet Rahul Sikri

Surekha Sikri is survived by her son Rahul Sikri.

Rahul is the only known child of the late actress. He was born from Sikri’s first marriage. Likewise, Rahul is an artist based in Mumbai.

Surekha Sikri was married to her husband Hemant Rege.

Sadly, Rege died after suffering heart failure. He took his last breath on October 20, 2009.

Surekha Sikri passed away on a death date of July 16, 2021. Her death cause was disclosed as Cardiac arrest.

Apparently, the actress was suffering complications due to a brain stroke that she got in 2020. Previously, she had suffered a brain stroke in 2018 as well.

Surekha Sikri Net Worth

Surekha Sikri earned a decent net worth from her career, which is still under review.

Her first credited role was in 1978. She portrayed the role of Meera in the movie Kissa Kursi Ka as her debut role.

In 1989, Sikri had made her breakthrough as the Sangeet Natak Akademi Award winner. Moreover, she had also received Filmfare and Indian Television Academy awards.

She was a gem to Bollywood and Indian TV serials. Various celebrities have come forward to pay tribute to the late actress.

We send our thoughts and prayers to Surekha Sikri’s friends and family. May her soul rest in peace.
