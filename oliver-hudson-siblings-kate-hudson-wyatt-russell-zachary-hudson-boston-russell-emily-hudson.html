Oliver Hudson siblings: Kate Hudson, Wyatt Russell, Zachary Hudson, Boston Russell, Emily Hudson

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 30, 2024

Oliver Rutledge Hudson is an American actor. He gained notoriety for his roles as Adam Rhodes in the CBS comedy Rules of Engagement (2007–2013) and Wes Gardner in the Fox horror-comedy Scream Queens.

In the 2013–2015 season of the ABC musical drama Nashville, he also played Jeff Fordham.

He played a key role in the ABC comedy series Splitting Up Together, which was produced by Ellen DeGeneres.
He debuted in Fox’s The Cleaning Lady in 2022 as FBI agent Garrett Miller.

On June 9, 2006, Hudson married the actress Erinn Bartlett in Cabo San Lucas, Mexico. They have three children together.

He is an American actor with a fortune of $10 million. The birthplace of Oliver Hudson is Los Angeles.
