Who are Cody Rhodes Parents?

Cody Rhodes is an American professional wrestler who is presently signed to WWE. In this article, we explore Cody Rhodes parents and career.

Cody Rhodes Biography

On 30 June 1985, Cody Rhodes was born in Georgia in the United States, 

At the age of only 17, Cody has changed his birth name as ‘Cody Rhodes’. Cody Rhodes did his schooling from the ‘Lassiter High School’.

Cody was a great wrestler in high school. Cody Rhodes did his college from the ‘Pennsylvania State University’. As a junior wrestler, Cody has won the ‘Georgia State Tournament’ with much success and honour.

With this, Cody decided to pursue wrestling as his professional career. Later after his graduation, Cody Rhodes enrolled himself in an acting school.

As of January 2021, The total estimated net worth of Cody Rhodes is about $5 million approximately.

He has an annual income of $3 million and even earns more from his endorsements and merchandise.

He has won the WWE Tag Team Championship twice, once with Drew McIntyre and once with his half-brother Goldust. He is also a three-time World Tag Team Champion.

Cody Rhodes is a magnificent professional and a powerful wrestler who has established a successful career in the wrestling fraternity. In addition to this, Cody Rhodes is also a great actor who has been seen in a number of movies and television series, respectively. Cody Rhodes is best known by his ring name as ‘The American Nightmare’.

Cody Rhodes was also honoured by the heavyweight title as ‘One-time ROH World Champion’. In a way, Cody Rhodes belonged to a wrestling family where his late father ‘Dusty Rhodes’ was a WWE Hall of Fame of his time. Cody Rhodes’s half-brother ‘Goldust’ was also a WWE wrestler.

Cody Rhodes resides with his spouse Brandi in a comfortable abode situated in Charlotte, North Carolina.

Cody and Brandi Rhodes announced their engagement in November 2012 and tied the knot one year later in September 2013.

The two have been married for 11 years now and in 2021, they welcomed their first child, a daughter who they named Liberty Runnels.

Who are Cody Rhodes Parents?

He comes from a lineage of accomplished wrestlers, with his father being the renowned WWE Superstar Dusty Rhodes and his brother, Dustin (Goldust), also a professional wrestler. Cody and his partner Brandi recently became parents on June 18, 2022.
