Biography

Reza Pahlavi was born in Tehran, Iran on October 31, 1960. Better known as the Crown Prince of Iran, he was the last heir apparent to the throne. He is also known for being the founder and former leader of the National Council of Iran. He is also known for having founded his own television network called Ofogh Iran. He was named as the unofficial godfather of Princess Louise of Belgium in 2004. He studied at a school located in the royal palace and later trained as a pilot, making his first solo flight at the age of 11. He served as a cadet in the Imperial Iranian Air Force and enrolled in The American University in Cairo where he earned a degree in political science.
