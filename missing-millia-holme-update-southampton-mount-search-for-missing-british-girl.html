Missing Millia Holme Update: Southampton Mount Search For Missing British Girl

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, July 21, 2024

 Missing Millia Holme: Southampton Mounts Search for British Teen

The Southampton community is in distress as 14-year-old Millia Holme has been missing since July 18, 2023. Her sudden disappearance has left family, friends, and neighbors deeply concerned. Known for her responsibility, Millia’s vanishing act is highly unusual, prompting a community-wide search and authorities’ involvement.


Millia Holme, a 14-year-old girl from Southampton, UK, is described as having long brown hair, hazel eyes, and stands approximately 5 feet 4 inches tall. On the day of her disappearance, she was dressed in a light blue jacket, black trousers, and white sneakers. Her last known location was near her Southampton residence.

The authorities have initiated an active search and investigation to locate Millia and ensure her safety. The community has rallied together, with volunteers organizing search parties and distributing flyers. A strong social media presence, characterized by the trending hashtag #FindMilliaHolme, has united the nation in the search effort. Your assistance is invaluable in the search for Millia Holme. If you possess any information about her whereabouts or have seen her after July 18, 2023, please promptly reach out to the Southampton Police Department at 631-728-5000 or the Missing Persons Hotline at 101[10]. Your cooperation and involvement are deeply appreciated.
