Marney Gellner Biography and Wiki

A Langdon, North Dakota native, Marney Gellner is a journalist who is known for working for Bally Sports North. She is married to Matt Mathiasen and is a graduate of the University of Mary and North Dakota State University.

In her role as the network’s host, Marney Gellner is a prominent figure, captivating audiences with her sports expertise and engaging presence. Her journey in the sports broadcasting world has been filled with exciting experiences and remarkable achievements.

Before joining her current station, Marney made her mark at Fox Sports North, where she showcased her talent as an anchor, sideline reporter, and host. Her dynamic skills were evident as she provided insightful sideline reports for iconic teams like the Minnesota Twins and Timberwolves. Notably, she also took on the role of play-by-play announcer for the Minnesota Lynx, showcasing her versatility in the sports media landscape.

Prior to these accomplishments, Marney started her broadcasting career at KXMB-TV in Bismarck, North Dakota, where she served as a weekend sports anchor and sports reporter. Her passion for sports reporting was evident from the beginning, and her dedication to delivering top-notch coverage led her to further success.

After making a significant impact in North Dakota, Marney’s journey continued to flourish as she joined WDAZ-TV as their weekend sports anchor and reporter. Her talent and charisma were recognized, leading her to a position at Channel 3 as their esteemed sports reporter.

Throughout her career, Marney has earned a well-deserved reputation for her insightful reporting and engaging storytelling. Her dedication to sports journalism has been a driving force in her journey, making her a respected figure in the sports broadcasting community.
