His Career Began On YouTube on January 7, 2015

Cody started his independent professional life as a YouTuber on January 11, 2015, by uploading a single video where he showed off highlights of his 2014 Bow season where he tagged a 150 inch whitetail – this only garnered around 254k views. His first viral video was on August 8, 2019 with a video entitled “Fourwheeler on REAPER WHEELS literally tills dirt,” which attracted over 16 million viewers. 

As expected, he pivoted his video content and started modifying/destroying/testing various types of vehicles and capturing the results in video format.

How Much Does He Make?

After years of putting effort into his social media profiles, especially YouTube, WhistlinDiesel’s net worth (as of now) is around $3.4 million. Most of his income comes from his YouTube channel, but recently, it got released about how Cody also accumulates a heavy amount from other social media channels like Instagram.

His income is estimated by multiplying $3 to $5 per thousand video views (average RPM payout of YouTube) times his total of 680 million views to get to $3.4 million.
