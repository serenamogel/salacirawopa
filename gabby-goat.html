Gabby Goat | Enjoy Illinois

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, January 28, 2024

The Gabby Goat American Pub & Grill is the premiere restaurant and sports bar in Effingham, IL for your next night out, event, gathering, party, breakfast, lunch or dinner!

The Gabby Goat is all about a culmination of creativity, desire, passion and just plain old hard work. We are an American Pub and Grill. The driving force throughout our development has been and will always be to provide you, our guests, with the “Greatest Of All Times.” Breakfast, lunch and dinner are all served here with food and drink specialties every day. We offer foods like steaks, burgers, wraps, gourmet hot dogs, horseshoes, nachos and more. Take a seat at the bar, main dining area or beer garden, equipped with large TVs and a smoking section. Enjoy a game of pool or darts, and control the juke box for a fun night out. Eat, drink and get your goat on!
