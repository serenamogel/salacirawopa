8 Things You Didnt Know About Esha Deol

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, April 27, 2024

➡ Esha Deol Biography

Esha Deol Takhtani, popularly known as Esha Deol who is known for her outstanding acting skills as she grabbed the attention in her debut film ‘Koi Mere Dil Se Pooche’ in which she shared the screen space alongside Aftab Shivdasani. The film was released in 2002 and earned a nomination for the Best Female Debut at the Filmfare Award ceremony. After giving a successful debut, she never looked back and continued her acting venture. However, there are many things that people don’t know about the quintessential actress and we are sure her fans also want to know more about her. So, let us have a look at these unknown things!

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Esha Deol

1- Comes from a family of Actors

Esha Deol is the daughter of the renowned, veteran actors, Dharmendra and Hema Malini who later turned into Politicians. While her brothers, Sunny Deol, Bobby Deol, and Abhay Deol are also actors and gained much fame.

2- Married to Businessman

People including her fans assumed that the gorgeous actress will marry any celebrity as celebrities have relationships with each other, but the star shocked the nation when she married Bharat Takhtani who is a successful businessman.

3- Beauty with Brains

The actress is known for not just her acting skills, yet her high intelligence as well. She pursued a degree of bachelors in the field of Computer Technology from Oxford University. And later, did Masters in Arts.

4- Pleasant Moment

Esha Deol once shared her life’s pleasant moment. It was the time when she got the chance to work alongside Bollywood biggies in her debut film titled ‘Koi Mere Dilse Pooche’ in which she worked alongside Jaya Bachchan, Anupam Kher, and Sanjay Kapoor. While Aftab Shivdasani appeared as a lead.

5- Love & Appreciation

The actress got extreme love and appreciation for giving a remarkable performance in the Bollywood debut titled ‘Koi Mere Dil Se Pooche’ which was among the best releases of 2002. Even since then, she gained a massive fan following.

6- Worked with Hrithik Roshan

Eshal Deol got a chance to work alongside the heartthrob actor, Hrithik Roshan who is the crush of many girls, but fortunately, Esha was approached to share the screen with him in their movie named ‘Na Tum Jano Na Hum’ in which Saif Ali Khan appeared as a supporting actor.

7- Rose to Fame

Esha Deol rose to fame after appearing in another Bollywood movie named ‘Dhoom Machale’ in which she also performed on an item song. In the film, her dusky look grabbed the hearts of many. Undoubtedly, she did an amazing job!

8- Dancing Diva

She and her mom, Hema Malini is known for their dancing skills. In fact, Esha is a well-trained dancer as she got trained in the Odissi dancing style under Mumbai guru Rabindra Atibuddhi. Apart from this, she is trained in Bharatnatyam as well.
