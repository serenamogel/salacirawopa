Meet His Daughter Madison Hunter Family

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 28, 2024

People are curious to know about David Hunter wife and his other family members after the community champion passed away at 48.

David Hunter, also known as Dave, was a highly regarded advocate for Windsor-Essex. His impact on the lives of individuals throughout the region was profound.

The prominent figure known for his community work was discovered deceased on the morning of June 30, 2023.

His daughter, Madison, shared the heartbreaking news of his passing with the media.

David dedicated himself to numerous charitable initiatives and even established a magazine. Regrettably, at the age of 48, he left this world.

The sudden loss of the community champion has left those who knew him in shock.

In the wake of his untimely demise, social media platforms have been inundated with tributes and condolences from countless individuals expressing their sorrow.

However, the exact circumstances surrounding Hunter’s passing have made people wonder about his family. Let’s look into that.

Meet David Hunter Wife Shawna Pelletier

David Hunter was married and had a beautiful wife. On Friday, his wife and children, who deeply loved him, took to social media to confirm the heartbreaking news.

Expressing her deep emotions, his wife Shawna Pelletier wrote on her social media, “I don’t know how to live without you… I love you so much, Dave.”

The family has chosen not to disclose the cause of his death, and details regarding funeral arrangements have yet to be announced.

Even though there is not much information regarding his wife, Hunter was loved abundantly by his family and community.

Originally from Corunna, Ontario, he completed his education at Sarnia Collegiate Institute and Technical School.

With his versatility and willingness to assist others, Hunter became recognized for his extensive community involvement.

David Hunter Family: His Daughter Madison Hunter 

David Hunter had a daughter named Madison Hunter. She was the one who broke the news about the beloved community champion passing away at the age of 48.

Besides his wife and daughter, David reportedly is survived by his grandchildren; however, the information about his family has not been discussed in detail.

His daughter, Madison recounts that on Friday morning, she woke up to discover six missed calls from her stepmother on her phone.

Upon returning the call, she received the devastating news that her father, Dave Hunter, had passed away peacefully in his sleep.

In an interview taken in Windsor, Ont. on Sunday, July 2, 2023, with CTV News, Madison extensively discusses how she learned about her father’s passing.

Furthermore, she also read tributes from individuals whose lives were touched by him and the coping process for her family.

Dave Hunter was widely recognized as a prominent figure in Windsor-Essex, actively involved in charitable events and impacting numerous lives throughout the region.

The man was honored with the Young Entrepreneur of the Year title by the Windsor-Essex Regional Chamber of Commerce.

Furthermore, Dave launched “The Drive” magazine, releasing its inaugural issue in December 2000.

Aside from his community engagements, Hunter, a local realtor, was also involved in managing the sale of the Low-Martin Mansion in Walkerville.

The property is currently listed with an asking price of $3.4 million. Now that David has left us all, it will be someone’s else responsibility to manage the deal.
