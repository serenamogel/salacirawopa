Discovered by the late great Prince, DaniLeigh (pronounced Danny Lay) is a talented singer and choreographer. Hailing from South Florida, she traveled to Los Angeles, intending to "make it." She was only sixteen at the time. 

Having landed a few notable dancing/singing gigs, DaniLeigh eventually skyrocketed to national superstardom with her gold-certified single Lil Bebe. Continuing on her career trajectory, she then released her debut album entitled The Plan.

Released in November 2018, The Plan was DaniLeigh's coming-of-age tale. In the LP, she paid fitting homage to her heritage and family.

Bio - Ethnicity & Parents

With the real name Danielle Leigh Curiel, DaniLeigh was born in 1994 to parents Vladimir (father) and Vicky Curiel (mother). Her biological parents were from the Dominican Republic. She was raised in Miami, Florida. 

Still connected to her Dominican roots, Danileigh belonging to Hispanic ethnicity. A bilingual performer, she is proficient in both English and Spanish.

She was deeply into music from a young age. By the time she was 12, she had already started dancing, and at the age of 12, she had started singing. However, initially, she was shy to show her singing talent until she got positive feedbacks. 

And having loving parents by her side, the Easy hitmaker's family supported her artistic aspirations. To help her realize her show-biz dreams, her parents left their booming restaurant business in Orlando and moved to LA.

Although she had already posted cover songs of Musiq Soulchild's 'So Beautiful' on YouTube, her career didn't officially start until she moved to LA. 

She then started as a background dancer for artists, including Pharrell Williams and Nelly Furtado. She had even formed a pop dancing duo with her sister Curly Fryz. 

Talent seems to run in the 26 years old songstress' family. Her three siblings - sisters Amanda & Brianna and a brother - are in some capacity involved with the show-biz.
