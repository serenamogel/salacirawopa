Best Ice Cream: 17 Best Ice Creams from Our Taste Tests

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, January 19, 2024

Sure, we all scream for ice cream, but we really holler our heads off for the best ice cream. We have tasted a lot of ice cream since we launched way back in 2022, so, to help guide you through the frozen treats aisle, we’ve compiled all the best ice creams we’ve tried so far.

Here’s how we made this list: Each one of these ice cream flavors took the top spot within a separate ranking. And each one of those rankings was decided through rigorous taste tests in which we pitted the best ice cream brands against one another. We prized pure flavors, creamy textures, and quality of mix-ins, when appropriate. So, you’re really getting the best of the best ice cream flavors with this list. Whether you’re looking for the best classic vanilla or the apex of Ben & Jerry’s achievements, you’ll find the best grocery store ice cream here. Get your spoons and your vocal cords ready for some serious screaming over the best ice cream.

Best Vanilla

Häagen-Dazs Vanilla Bean Ice CreamWalmart

In our vanilla ice cream ranking, Sporked’s Jordan Myrick wrote, “It’s incredible. Like, it’s the absolutely perfect and most delicious vanilla ice cream you can imagine.” The vanilla bean-speckled ice cream is sweet (but not too sweet), extremely silky and creamy, and shockingly complex, considering it’s a simple pint of vanilla ice cream. If you’re looking for the best ice cream to scoop onto a slice of apple pie or to use as a base in an ice cream sundae, or maybe you’re just a vanilla purist, then Haagen-Dazs is the best ice cream brand for you.

Best Chocolate

Straus Family Creamery Organic Dutch Chocolate Ice CreamInstacart

You really can’t do better than Straus when it comes to the best chocolate ice cream. It’s extremely smooth and light—but still deeply chocolatey. It tastes a bit like a more solid version of the best chocolate milkshake you’ve ever had. Straus is a bit of a premium brand, but it’s worth the extra cash to secure the best grocery store ice cream for chocolate lovers.

Best Cookies & Cream

Dreyer’s / Edy’s Cookies ‘N Cream Ice CreamVonsWalmart

Jordan admitted to judging Dreyer’s by its packaging (“the carton looks so plain compared to some of the other, flashier brands on the market,” they wrote), but upon tasting they quickly learned that you can’t tell the best ice cream brands by their labels. Dreyer’s Cookies N’ Cream (aka Edy’s Cookies N’ Cream, depending on where you are in the U.S.) is creamy and packed with cookie chunks. Plus, it’s affordable.

Best Mint Chocolate Chip

Straus Organic Mint Chocolate Chip Ice CreamInstacart

“If you’re looking for a high-quality, well-made, impressive mint chip ice cream, look no further than Straus Organic Mint Chocolate Chip,” Jordan wrote in their review. This is an admittedly fancy mint chip ice cream, but you deserve it. The chocolate chips are perfectly distributed and the mint flavor is pure and not toothpaste-y. If you’re looking for an upgrade on a childhood favorite, this is the best ice cream to buy.

Best Coffee

Ben & Jerry’s Coffee, Coffee BuzzBuzzBuzz!Instacart

This coffee ice cream is just as intense as its name suggests. Each bite is packed with earthy, slightly bitter coffee flavor. And the ice cream itself is super rich, thanks to a high percentage of butterfat. That helps temper the bold coffee flavor and keep you coming back for spoonful after spoonful. But that’s not all. Ben & Jerry’s is probably the best ice cream brand for mix-in freaks, and they maintain their reputation with this coffee ice cream. There are big ol’ chunks of fudge throughout and they are absolutely delightful.

Best Strawberry

Tillamook Oregon Strawberry Ice CreamTarget

Tangy, tart, fresh strawberry flavor meets creamy, delicious dairy in this ice cream, which took the top spot in our best strawberry ice cream ranking. There are even big chunks of strawberry (actual strawberry!) in the mix. Take a bite, shut your eyes, and let the home-churned flavor of Tillamook’s strawberry ice cream wash over you. This was the best ice cream by far in our strawberry ice cream taste test.

Best Ben & Jerry’s Flavor

Peanut Butter Half BakedTarget

As we’ve mentioned before, Ben & Jerry’s is the best ice cream brand if you’re really mostly interested in the mix-ins. And the best Ben & Jerry’s flavor that is simply packed with sweet treats is Peanut Butter Half Baked. The base is a blend of chocolate and peanut butter ice cream stirred together with chunks of peanut butter cookie dough and fudge brownie hunks. It’s salty. It’s sweet. It’s totally indulgent. It’s the best ice cream to eat by yourself while crying.

Best Dairy-Free

Ben & Jerry’s Phish Food Non-Dairy Frozen DessertTarget

Even if you can handle dairy, you will love this non-dairy version of fan favorite Ben & Jerry’s ice cream, Phish Food. It tastes almost exactly the same. And it’s still packed with all those good, B&J signature mix-ins. Made with almond milk, it’s shockingly creamy (so many vegan ice creams are way too icy). It really needs to be tried to be believed. Vegans, this is the best grocery store ice cream for you.
