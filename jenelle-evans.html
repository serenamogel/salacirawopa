Jenelle Evans is an American TV personality and social media influencer who is best known for having been cast in the reality television series, Teen Mom 2, alongside Chelsea DeBoer, Kailyn Lowry, and Leah Messer. Apart from that, she has also been a part of a couple of other projects such as The Playboy Morning Show, 16 and Pregnant, Happy Death Day, and The Doctors.
