Who are Tyran Moores parents as he stars on Growing Up Hip Hop?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, June 10, 2024

The year 2022 has brought back the excitement of “Growing Up Hip Hop,” with Season 7 kicking off on January 6th. The familiar faces of the show, including Angela Simmons, Jojo, Tahira “Tee Tee” Francis, and Egypt Criss, are back for another thrilling season.One name that’s been in the spotlight since Season 4 is Tyran Moore, who has made a return to “Growing Up Hip Hop” in 202In this article, we delve into the world of Tyran Moore, focusing on his family background, age, Instagram presence, and his unique career path.

Who is Tyran Moore?

Tyran Moore is a prominent cast member on the popular show “Growing Up Hip Hop.” Born in 1990, he is approximately 32 years old, adding youthful energy to the series.What makes Tyran’s story even more intriguing is his family lineage. Tyran is the son of Sandra Jacqueline Denton, also known as Pepa from the iconic rap group Salt-N-Pepa. This musical connection runs deep in the family, as his half-sister, Egypt Criss, also shares the screen with him and their mother on “Growing Up Hip Hop.”According to Heavy, Tyran’s father is Barry Riddick, who had his own rap career under the name Tyran “Tah-Tah” Moore. This lineage of musical talent and influence has certainly shaped Tyran’s journey.

Tyran’s Diverse Career

Despite being born into a family of musicians, Tyran has chosen a different path for himself. He has ventured into the world of business and martial arts, showcasing his versatility and determination.Throughout his appearances on “Growing Up Hip Hop,” fans have witnessed Tyran’s entrepreneurial spirit as he launched his first Kung Fu School. Additionally, in 2020, he expressed his aspirations to explore new acting roles and engage in competitive martial arts.One striking aspect of Tyran’s personality is his ability to stay away from the drama often associated with reality TV. His passion for Kung Fu reveals his profound self-discipline and unwavering motivation. As he put it in 2020, “This is my life, it’s not just physical. Any spiritual or physical problem I have, this is my go-to. It shows you what you’re truly capable of.”

Connecting with Tyran on Instagram

In the age of social media, Tyran Moore has established a notable presence on Instagram, where he engages with his followers. With over 20,000 followers, you can find him under the handle @tysensei07.A quick glance at his Instagram bio reveals his commitment to principles of equality and individuality. He writes, “I’m about equality, being my own unique individual, expressing myself to the fullest! I want you all to be EQUALLY UNIQUE!”A deeper look into his Instagram feed showcases his unwavering passion for martial arts. Almost all of his posts resonate with this lifelong pursuit, demonstrating his dedication to this ancient discipline.

Conclusion

As Tyran Moore returns to “Growing Up Hip Hop” in 2022, his journey continues to captivate audiences. Born into a family with a rich musical heritage, Tyran has forged his own unique path as an entrepreneur and martial arts enthusiast. His dedication to Kung Fu and his ability to stay grounded amidst the drama of reality TV make him a compelling figure in the entertainment world. With a thriving Instagram presence, Tyran connects with fans who share his passion for martial arts and individuality, solidifying his status as a rising star in his own right.
