Who is Cameron Newton?

Cameron Jerrell Newton was an American football quarterback who is currently a free agent. He earned the nickname "Super Cam" holds the NFL records for career quarterback rushing touchdowns and is second in career quarterback rushing yards. After a stint with the University of Florida, Newton played college football at Auburn University, where he won the Heisman Trophy and the 2011 BCS National Championship Game as a junior. In the 2011 NFL Draft, he was selected as the first overall pick by the Carolina Panthers.

Newton made a significant impact in his rookie season, setting records for passing and rushing yards by a quarterback, which earned him the Offensive Rookie of the Year award. He was the first rookie quarterback in the league's history to throw for 4,000 yards in a season and the first to throw for 400 yards in his NFL debut. Additionally, he set the single-season record for quarterback rushing touchdowns.

From 2013 to 2017, Newton led the Panthers to four playoff appearances and three division titles.
