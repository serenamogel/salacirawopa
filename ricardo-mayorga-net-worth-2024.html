Ricardo Mayorga’s Early Life

Mayorga was born in a poverty-stricken area of Managua, Nicaragua. Despite the hardships, he found solace in boxing and started his professional career in 1993. His early life experiences shaped his aggressive boxing style and his determination to succeed.

Boxing Career

Mayorga’s boxing career is filled with notable victories and unforgettable matches. He has won multiple world championships in two weight classes, including the unified WBA (Super), WBC, Ring magazine, and lineal welterweight titles in 2003, and the WBC super welterweight title from 2005 to 2006.

Notable Fights

Some of Mayorga’s most notable fights include his bouts against Andrew ‘Six Heads’ Lewis, Vernon Forrest, and Oscar De La Hoya. These fights not only increased his popularity but also significantly boosted his earnings.

End of Boxing Career

Mayorga officially retired from professional boxing in 2019. His last fight was against Lester Martinez, a match that unfortunately ended in a loss for Mayorga. Despite the defeat, his career remains one of the most remarkable in the history of boxing.
